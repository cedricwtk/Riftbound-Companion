RIFTBOUND COMPANION

A mobile companion app for Riftbound players on iOS and Android.
Track matches, build decks, browse cards, and manage trades -- all in one app.

KEY FEATURES
- Match Score Tracker: Real-time Bo1/Bo3 scoring with legend and battlefield selection
- Match History: Win/loss log with win rate stats and match editing
- Card Browser: Full searchable card database with domain, type, and rarity filters
- Deck Builder: Build decks with rule enforcement, domain filtering, and analytics
- Trade Manager: Schedule trades, set reminders, and share trade cards via text or image

MONETIZATION
Currently free for all users. A planned one-time premium purchase will unlock
unlimited match history, unlimited deck slots, deck analytics, advanced stats,
and matchup tracking.
