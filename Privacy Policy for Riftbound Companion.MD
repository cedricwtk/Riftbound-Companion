Privacy Policy for Riftbound Companion

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Overview
Riftbound Companion is a companion app for Riftbound players. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it's used.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device
All user data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This includes:

Your display name
Match history and scores
Deck configurations
Trade records (including meetup details and agreed prices)
E-transfer email (if you choose to provide one)
App settings and preferences
This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share a trade card.

In-App Purchases
We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect:

Anonymous app user IDs
Purchase transaction data
Device type and OS version
RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Notifications
If you enable trade reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect personal information
We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
We do not share data with advertisers
We do not have user accounts or servers
We do not access your contacts, camera, or location
Sharing Features
When you share a trade card, the image or text is generated locally and shared through your device's native share sheet. We have no access to what you share or with whom.

Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
